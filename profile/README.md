## CIMR L2PAD
The Level-2 Product Algorithm Development (L2PAD) project is a four-year (2023-2027) activity initiated by the European Space Agency (ESA) to prepare Level-2 algorithms and Ground Processing Prototype (GPP) for the Copernicus Imaging Microwave Radiometer (CIMR) mission.

Please visit our webpage [l2pad.cimr.eu](https://l2pad.cimr.eu) to learn more about our project and access (draft) versions of our results.

We are looking forward to creating a welcoming, supportive and diverse community of enthusiasts, experts, and users
around the Copernicus Imaging Microwave Radiometer (CIMR) satellite mission. The primary focus of this community
is to develop and improve algorithms and prototype ground-processing software for the CIMR Level-2 products.

You are welcome to visit our [Discussions page](https://github.com/orgs/CIMR-L2PAD/discussions) to say
'hi' and ask any questions.

*Important:* This is a community we build together. Please be welcoming, open minded, and respectful in your interactions, and adhere
to our [code of conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
